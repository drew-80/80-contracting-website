Our Story

80 Contracting was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional contracting services that homeowners can trust. We believe in doing the job right the first time, every time.

With years of experience in the industry, our team brings expertise, professionalism, and dedication to every project. We treat your home as if it were our own, ensuring the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on materials or workmanship
  • Integrity: Honest pricing and transparent communication
  • Reliability: We show up on time and complete projects on schedule
  • Safety: Licensed, insured, and committed to safe work practices
